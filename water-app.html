Water Bill App

See, in plain numbers, what a household draws — the use that is metered to the litre and has a charge already designed for it — against the industrial draw that carries no obligation to disclose anything at all.

Free to copy and adapt. Every figure is sourced; the excess-use charge is a designed model, not a current bill.
